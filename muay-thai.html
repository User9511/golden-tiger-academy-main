Mauy Thai

Muay Thai, revered for its striking and clinching techniques, holds sway in combat sports across the globe, including MMA (Mixed Martial Arts). Renowned trainers and fighters have elevated its effectiveness, allowing practitioners to excel in various arenas. With its arsenal of powerful strikes, devastating knees, and precise clinch work, Muay Thai has solidified its position as a premier striking art within the realm of MMA. It empowers individuals of all backgrounds, offering not just physical fitness but also mental resilience and self-confidence.

Instructor

At Golden Tiger Academy, we offer two Muay Thai classes each week, taking place on Mondays and Thursdays. Led by our experienced instructor, Paul Wright, a former European champion in Muay Thai, these classes provide a comprehensive training experience for individuals aged 16 and above.

Muay Thai Class

Our Muay Thai classes kick off with an energizing warm-up, followed by technique training involving pads and partner drills suitable for all experience levels. We also delve into Muay Boran, the precursor to Muay Thai, adding depth to your training. Sparring sessions provide an opportunity to test and refine your skills in a supportive environment. Join us for dynamic warm-ups, focused technique drills, and friendly sparring matches to begin your Muay Thai journey today.

For Your First Class

Dress Comforably

Wear regular comfortable gym clothes without any zippers or metal accessories.

Stay Hydrated

Bring along a water bottle to keep yourself hydrated during training.

Safety First

Remove jewelry and trim your fingernails and toenails. This reduces the risk of accidental injury
