Fundamentals Class

Start your BJJ journey with our fundamentals class every Monday. Perfect for newcomers, this session covers all the fundamental moves and principles of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. No prior martial arts experience is required, and there's no sparring involved. Plus, it's a no-gi class, so there's no need for a BJJ GI (uniform).

Principles & Rolling

On Thursdays, we offer another class suitable for beginners and beyond. This session focuses on BJJ Principles, where techniques and fundamental principles are taught. The class concludes with light rolling sessions, providing new students with the opportunity to gain comfort and experience in rolling. This is a GI class.

Advanced Sparring Class

On Tuesdays, we have an advanced sparring class. This No-Gi sparring session is exclusively for students who have achieved at least a two-stripe white belt in BJJ.

Kids / Teens Classes

Our classes for kids and teens ensure a safe and enjoyable space for them to learn Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, promoting the cultivation of confidence, discipline, and physical fitness in a fun and engaging manner.

For Your First Class

Dress Comforably

Wear regular comfortable gym clothes without any zippers or metal accessories.

Stay Hydrated

Bring along a water bottle to keep yourself hydrated during training.

Safety First

Please remove any jewelry and trim your fingernails and toenails. This reduces the risk of injury and accidental scratches.
