Personal Training

Looking for a way to make the most of your time while your kids train downstairs? Join certified Personal Trainer Kalum Byrne upstairs for a tailored fitness session designed to help you achieve your goals. With over 15 years of martial arts experience, Kalum brings unique insight into functional movement, strength, and conditioning. Whether you want to improve strength, boost energy, or fit a workout into your busy schedule, he will guide you through safe and effective exercises in a welcoming and supportive environment.

About Your Trainer

Kalum Byrne is a fully qualified Personal Trainer with a QQI Level 5 Certificate in Sports, Recreation and Exercise and internationally recognised ITEC certifications in Fitness Instructing and Personal Training. With 15+ years of martial arts training and experience in functional movement, strength, and conditioning, Kalum designs programs that are both effective and safe for all fitness levels. His mission is simple: to help you feel stronger, healthier, and more confident—one session at a time.

Why Train While Your Kids Train?

Parents often struggle to find time for themselves so why not work out while your child trains? Instead of waiting around, you can enjoy a focused personal training session upstairs. These sessions are convenient, time-efficient, and tailored to your individual fitness goals. Kalum’s martial arts experience also brings unique insights into balance, coordination, and functional strength, making your workout even more effective.
