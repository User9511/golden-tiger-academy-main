Korean Kickboxing

Korean style kickboxing is a dynamic kicking art emphasizees quick strikes and strategic footwork, fostering effective self-defence skills. Korean kickboxing is influenced by various martial arts styles including Won Hwa Do, Taekwondo and kickboxing. With its focus on versatility and practicality, Korean kickboxing equips practitioners with the tools needed to succeed both in sparring matches and real-world encounters.

For Your First Class

Dress Comforably

Wear regular comfortable gym clothes without any zippers or metal accessories.

Stay Hydrated

Bring along a water bottle to keep yourself hydrated during training.

Safety First

Remove jewelry and trim your fingernails and toenails. This reduces the risk of accidental injury
