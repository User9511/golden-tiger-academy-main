Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ)

What is BJJ? Learn the Basics of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) focuses on grappling and ground fighting, using technique and leverage over strength. Known for its effectiveness in self-defense and MMA, BJJ teaches holds, chokes, and joint locks to control opponents. Learn BJJ in both gi and no-gi styles, improving fitness, mental toughness, and confidence.
