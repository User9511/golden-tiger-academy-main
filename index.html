About Us

Ken Byrne Head Instructor

Golden Tiger Academy, founded in 2008 by Ken Byrne, our Head Instructor who has over 38 years of martial arts experience and has been awarded a black belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Kickboxing, Kenpo Karate, Kantanni Karate, WonHwaDo and Haidong Gumdo.

At Golden Tiger Academy we have classes that are designed to improve your self-defense and fitness levels. We have classes for all ages kids, teens and adults. Golden Tiger Academy provides a welcoming and supportive environment to train in to help you progress in your martial arts journey.

Our Classes

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

BJJ focuses on ground grappling and submissions. Develop self-defense skills, fitness, and mental resilience through technical groundwork. Suitable for all skill levels.

Learn More about Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Muay Thai

Muay Thai, the 'Art of Eight Limbs,' integrates punches, kicks, elbows, and knee strikes. Known for its power and effectiveness, it builds strength and striking precision.

Learn More about Muay Thai

Kenpo Karate

Kenpo Karate blends punches, kicks, and blocks for self-defense. Discipline, respect, and physical conditioning are core aspects, fostering personal development and martial skill.

Learn More about Kenpo Karate

Kickboxing

Kickboxing combines punches and kicks for a dynamic striking art. Cardiovascular fitness, agility, and strong kicks characterize this sport, promoting overall physical conditioning.

Learn More about Kickboxing

Korean Kickboxing

Korean style kickboxing is a dynamic kicking art and is influenced by various martial arts styles including Won Hwa Do, Taekwondo and kickboxing.

Learn More about Korean Kickboxing
